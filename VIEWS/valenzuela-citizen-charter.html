VALENZUELA CITIZEN CHARTER

City of Valenzuela, Metropolitan Manila

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

A charter establishing the rights, responsibilities, and commitments of the City Government of Valenzuela towards its citizens.

Preamble

The City of Valenzuela, in recognition of its obligations to serve the public interest and promote the general welfare, hereby establishes this Citizen Charter to clearly define the rights and responsibilities of both the city government and its citizens. This charter is founded on the principles of transparency, accountability, efficiency, and citizen participation.

I. RIGHTS OF CITIZENS

  1. Right to Public Information: Citizens have the right to access public information held by the City Government, subject to legal restrictions on national security and privacy.
  2. Right to Transparent Government: Citizens have the right to know how their tax money is spent and how government decisions are made.
  3. Right to Participate: Citizens have the right to participate in public consultations, hearings, and decision-making processes that affect their community.
  4. Right to Quality Public Services: Citizens are entitled to receive efficient, courteous, and responsive public services from city government agencies.
  5. Right to Fair Treatment: Citizens have the right to be treated fairly and without discrimination in all government transactions.
  6. Right to Access Justice: Citizens have the right to appeal government decisions and lodge complaints through established grievance mechanisms.
  7. Right to Privacy: Citizens' personal information held by the government is protected and used only for authorized purposes.
  8. Right to Safe and Clean Environment: Citizens have the right to live in a safe, healthy, and environmentally sustainable community.

II. RESPONSIBILITIES OF CITIZENS

  1. Compliance with Laws: Citizens must comply with national and local laws and regulations.
  2. Payment of Taxes and Fees: Citizens are responsible for paying taxes and authorized government fees in a timely manner.
  3. Community Participation: Citizens are encouraged to actively participate in community development and governance.
  4. Environmental Stewardship: Citizens must respect and protect the city's natural resources and environment.
  5. Peaceful Conduct: Citizens must maintain peace and order and refrain from violence and harassment.
  6. Honest Dealing: Citizens must engage in honest and truthful dealings with government agencies.
  7. Reporting Violations: Citizens are encouraged to report corruption, illegal activities, and violations of this charter to appropriate authorities.

III. COMMITMENTS OF THE CITY GOVERNMENT

  1. Service Quality: The City Government commits to provide public services with efficiency, quality, and transparency within established service standards.
  2. Accountability: All government officials and employees shall be accountable for their actions and subject to performance evaluation.
  3. Anti-Corruption: The City Government is committed to preventing and prosecuting corruption in all forms.
  4. Public Consultation: The City Government shall conduct regular public consultations on major policies and development projects.
  5. Financial Transparency: All government budgets and expenditures shall be made publicly available and subject to audit.
  6. Responsive Administration: Government agencies shall respond to citizen inquiries and complaints within prescribed timeframes.
  7. Inclusive Development: The City Government commits to ensure that development programs benefit all sectors of society, especially the marginalized.
  8. Disaster Preparedness: The City Government shall maintain preparedness for natural disasters and emergency situations.

IV. SERVICE STANDARDS

Service Standard Response Time
Business Permit Issuance 5 working days
Birth Certificate Issuance 3 working days
Complaint Resolution 15 working days
Information Request 7 working days

V. FEEDBACK AND COMPLAINTS

Citizens may submit complaints and feedback through the following channels:

  • City Mayor's Office - Direct Line
  • Online Feedback Portal - www.valenzuela.gov.ph
  • Email - feedback@valenzuela.gov.ph
  • In-Person at City Hall, Ground Floor, Citizen Services
  • Hotline: 1-888-VALENZUELA

VI. ENFORCEMENT AND REVIEW

This Charter shall be reviewed annually by the City Government in consultation with citizen representatives. Any violations of this charter by government officials or employees shall be subject to appropriate disciplinary action. Citizens who believe their rights have been violated may file a complaint with the Office of the Ombudsman or seek legal remedy through the courts.

VII. EFFECTIVE DATE

This Citizen Charter shall take effect upon its approval and shall remain in force until amended or repealed by proper authority.
